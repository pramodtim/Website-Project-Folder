About Me

Hi, I’m Pramod Timalsina – currently living in London and pursuing an MSc in Business Analytics at the University of West London. Before moving to the UK, I spent over nine years working in public administration in Nepal, leading teams, managing projects, and helping improve local services. It was a rewarding experience that taught me a lot about leadership, communication, and working with people from all walks of life.

Now, I’m diving deep into the world of data analytics — something I’ve always been curious about. I'm especially interested in how we can use data to solve real-world problems and make smarter decisions in business. I enjoy working with tools like Python, Tableau, EViews, MySQL, and Excel, and I’m building skills in data visualization, forecasting, and predictive modelling.

Outside of work and study, I enjoy traveling, learning about different cultures, and telling stories through data. I’m always open to new opportunities to learn, collaborate, and grow — especially in areas that bring together people, ideas, and technology.

Thanks for stopping by my page — feel free to get in touch!
