About Me

Hi, I’m Pramod Timalsina from Nepal — currently living in London and pursuing an MSc in Business Analytics at the University of West London. Previously, I spent over nine years in public administration, leading teams, managing projects, and improving local services.

Now I’m diving deep into data analytics. I work with Python, Tableau, EViews, MySQL and Excel; my interests include data visualisation, forecasting and predictive modelling.

Outside study and work, I enjoy travelling, learning cultures and telling stories through data. I’m always open to collaborations and learning opportunities.
